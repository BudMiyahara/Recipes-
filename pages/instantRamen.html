Instant Ramen

Ingriedients

  • Instant ramen packet
  • Water
  • Hot Pot
  • Chop Sticks

Instructions

  1. Pour water into hot pot and start boiling process
  2. While water is being heated, open ramen packet and put noodles in large bowl
  3. Take out flavoring packet and pour it over the noodles
  4. When water reaches a biol, pour water over the noodles until they float
  5. Use chop sticks to stir the noodles
  6. Stir until noodles are soft
  7. Enjoy
