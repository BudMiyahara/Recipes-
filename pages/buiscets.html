Biscuits

Ingriedients

  • Pillsbury biscuit dough
  • Oven
  • Cockie tray
  • Tin fiol

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. While oven pre-heats, wrap coockie tray in tin fiol
  3. Open biscuit dough and place biscuits on tray. Make sure none of them touch eachother
  4. When oven reaches temp, place the coockie tray into the oven
  5. Let bisuits bake for 8-11 minutes
  6. Afer time expires, take tray out of oven and let them rest
  7. Enjoy

Description

These are light and fluffy biscuits, perfect for breakfasts. Each one is sure to bring a smile to your face. It is easy to make and easy to share. One tin can make up to 8 biscuits.
