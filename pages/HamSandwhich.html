Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Ingriedients

  • White Bread
  • Honey Mustard
  • Honey Roasted Ham
  • American Cheese

Steps

  1. Toast bread
  2. Place one slice of bread on a plate and smear honey mustard onto it
  3. Place the american cheese onto the peice of bread
  4. Place 3 pieces of sliced ham onto the chees
  5. Add another peice of white bread to the stack
  6. Enjoy
Description

This is a traditional American dish.

I would say this is a lunch/dinner item for all income classes.

The combination of toasted bread combines nicely with cold cheese and ham.
