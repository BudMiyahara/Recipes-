Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Ingrierients

  • White Bread
  • Honey Mustard
  • Honey Roasted Ham
  • American Cheese

Steps

  1. Place one slice of bread on a plate and smear honey mustard onto it
  2. Place the american cheese onto the peice of bread
  3. Place 3 pieces of sliced ham onto the chees
  4. Add another peice of white bread to the stack
  5. Enjoy
